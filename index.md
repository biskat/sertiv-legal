# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 8, 2026

This policy explains what Sertiv collects about you, how it uses and
stores that information, who it's shared with, and the controls you
have. Plain language wherever possible.

## 1. Who runs Sertiv

Sertiv is operated by Tee Time LLC. We are the **data controller**
for personal information processed through the app. Questions, data
requests, or complaints: **support@sertiv.com**.

## 2. What we collect

| Category | Examples | How we get it |
|---|---|---|
| Account identity | Email address, user ID | You provide at sign-up. If you use Sign in with Apple, Apple may give us a private relay address (`@privaterelay.appleid.com`) instead of your real email — we never see the underlying address. |
| Profile preferences | Display name, coaching name, timezone, peak focus time, week-start day | You set in Settings |
| Your content | Goals, tasks, milestones, projects, life areas, notes, reminders | You create them in the app |
| Chat messages | Messages you send to Sertiv and the assistant's replies | From using the Chat tab |
| Calendar events | Event titles, times, durations, and locations from calendars you've granted Sertiv permission to read (Apple Calendar / iCloud / Google / Outlook accounts you've added to iOS) | Read on-device via Apple's EventKit, then mirrored to our database for use in coaching context. See §4. |
| Derived long-term memory | A rolling summary of older conversations and one-sentence notes about significant personal context you've shared | Generated by our AI provider from your messages |
| Product analytics | Anonymous event counts (screen opens, feature taps) keyed only to your user ID — no email, no message content, no personal data | Captured by the app when you interact with it |
| Device & usage (minimal) | App version, OS version | Collected for crash/error diagnostics only |

**What we do NOT collect:**
- Location
- Contacts
- Photos or media (unless you paste them into a message, in which case
  they travel with the message)
- Cross-app or cross-site tracking identifiers
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- Payment or financial information beyond what Apple processes for
  subscriptions — we never see your card details

## 3. How we use your data

We use your data exclusively to operate Sertiv:

- **Provide the coaching experience** — goals/tasks/etc. are what the
  assistant coaches you against, and your messages are what it
  responds to.
- **Personalize coaching** — the app derives a conversation summary and
  optional coaching notes so the assistant remembers context across
  sessions. See [CHAT_MEMORY.md](./CHAT_MEMORY.md) for the full
  architecture.
- **Operate and improve the app** — crash logs and minimal diagnostic
  data help us fix bugs.

We do **not**:
- Use your data for advertising
- Sell your data to anyone
- Train third-party AI models on your messages (see §5)
- Track you across apps or websites

## 4. AI processing (important)

When you use the Chat tab, your messages are sent to **Anthropic**
(https://www.anthropic.com), our AI provider, for processing by their
Claude large-language-model API. Anthropic acts as a **sub-processor**
on our behalf and:

- Receives the content of your messages plus a compiled summary of your
  goals, tasks, calendar events (when relevant to what you're asking
  about or planning), and other Sertiv context so the model can respond
  intelligently. Calendar event titles, times, and locations may be
  included in this context — they are not sent for any other purpose.
- Does **not** use your data to train their models by default under
  their API terms (zero-data-retention policy for API customers).
- Retains the content only for a short operational window per their
  stated policy, then deletes it.
- Anthropic's own privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy

We periodically run a lightweight secondary AI call against your
messages to derive (a) a short conversation summary and (b) one-
sentence notes about significant personal context. Both are stored on
your profile and shown to you in Settings → Chat memory, where you can
review, pause future capture, or delete anything that's been stored.

## 5. Sub-processors we use

| Provider | Role | What they receive |
|---|---|---|
| Anthropic | AI / LLM provider | Chat messages + a snapshot of your goals/tasks/calendar context per request |
| Supabase | Hosted database + authentication + edge functions | All data stored by the app (encrypted at rest, encrypted in transit) |
| PostHog | Product analytics | Anonymous event-level usage data keyed to your user ID — no email, no message content, no personal data. Used to understand which features people use so we can improve them. |
| Apple | Sign in with Apple, push notifications, App Store review (when applicable) | Standard platform metadata only; we never see your Apple ID password or device identifiers |

These providers are bound by industry-standard data-processing
agreements. We may add or change sub-processors with reasonable notice
via an updated version of this policy.

## 6. How long we keep your data

- **While your account is active**: indefinitely, so the app can keep
  working across sessions.
- **Soft-deleted chat history** (after you tap "Clear conversation
  history"): retained in the database with a `deleted_at` marker. Not
  shown in the UI. Not sent to the AI provider. Kept so you could
  theoretically request recovery — see §9.
- **After you delete your account**: everything you own is hard-deleted
  within seconds. Backups are retained by our hosting provider
  (Supabase) per their retention policy (typically up to 7 days for
  point-in-time recovery) and are then overwritten.
- **Abuse-prevention hash**: when your account is deleted, we retain a
  one-way SHA-256 hash of the normalized email address that was used,
  alongside the date your most recent trial was granted. This is not
  reversible into your email and contains no other data about you. We
  use it for a single purpose — to prevent the same email from
  receiving an unlimited number of free trials by repeated
  sign-up-and-delete. The hash is retained until our underlying trial
  model changes (e.g. when in-app purchases launch and trials move to
  Apple's introductory-offer system).

## 7. Children

Sertiv is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 where required by
law, including under the UK Age Appropriate Design Code and GDPR
Article 8). If we learn that we've collected personal data from a
child under that age, we'll delete it. Parents who believe their child
has provided us data should contact **support@sertiv.com**.

## 8. How we secure your data

- All traffic between your device and Sertiv servers is encrypted
  (TLS).
- Database rows are encrypted at rest by our hosting provider.
- Row-Level Security in the database ensures one user's data is never
  returned to another user's queries.
- Admin operations (e.g. deleting accounts) happen inside authenticated
  edge functions, never from the client.

No system is perfectly secure; if you suspect a breach has affected
you, contact **support@sertiv.com** and we'll investigate promptly.

## 9. Your rights

Regardless of where you live, you can:

- **Access / download** your data. Settings → Profile → **Export my
  data** produces a full JSON snapshot.
- **Correct** your profile information from Settings → Profile.
- **Delete** individual pieces of memory from Settings → Chat memory,
  clear your entire chat history with a soft-delete from the `···`
  menu in the Chat tab, or delete your entire account from Settings →
  Profile.
- **Opt out** of future AI-based memory extraction while keeping
  existing notes: Settings → Chat memory → "Pause new notes".
- **Object** to processing or request portability under GDPR / UK GDPR
  / CCPA.

If you're in the EU/UK/California, you also have the right to **lodge
a complaint** with your local data protection authority (ICO, CNIL,
CPPA, etc.).

Requests to exercise any of these rights: **support@sertiv.com**. We
aim to respond within 30 days.

## 10. International transfers

Data may be stored and processed in the United States (Supabase and
Anthropic both operate US-based infrastructure). If you are accessing
the app from the EU/UK, your data will be transferred to the US under
Standard Contractual Clauses or an equivalent lawful transfer
mechanism.

## 11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Substantive changes will be announced in
the app or via email to your account address. The "Effective date" at
the top will always reflect the current version.

## 12. Governing law

This policy is governed by the laws of the United States of America, without
reference to conflict-of-law rules. Nothing in this policy limits any
mandatory consumer-protection rights you have under the laws of your
country of residence.
